ABOUT US

We have a Person Centred Software for each resident. This software enables care staff to record care in real-time, enabling relatives to access care notes through a secure portal for relatives, thereby facilitating a two-way communication channel.

AS care services is constantly abreast of current legislation updates within the Care Act 2014, and this is instrumental in ensuring the Duty of Candour, which enables a transparent service delivery. AS Care services and their governing body, the Care Quality Commission (CQC), aim to ensure that social care providers provide a secure, caring, and high-quality care service to vulnerable adults.

Our objective is to provide the most comprehensive, person-centred care to all our residents, ensuring their comfort, security, and happiness. Our aim is to ensure that our homes are truly their home.

When individuals have a shared vision and values that resonate deeply, we are motivated to undertake significant initiatives to make it come true. Our vision encapsulates our core principles and values, which are applicable to every service we provide and to every stakeholder we interact with.

Our mission is to assist older individuals to live longer, healthier, more fulfilled lives, embody empathy and respect.
