ABOUT US

At AS Care Services, our mission is to help older individuals live longer, healthier, and more fulfilling lives. We are committed to providing person-centred care that places empathy, respect, and dignity at the core of everything we do.

Each resident benefits from our advanced Person Centred Software, allowing care staff to document care activities in real-time. This ensures transparency, as relatives can access care notes securely through a dedicated portal, enabling open two-way communication and peace of mind.

We remain fully compliant with the Care Act 2014 and uphold the Duty of Candour, ensuring honesty, accountability, and transparency in all care services. In partnership with our regulatory body, the Care Quality Commission (CQC), we strive to deliver safe, effective, and compassionate care to vulnerable adults.
