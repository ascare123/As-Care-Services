SWAN CARE HOME

Swan Care Home is pleasantly situated in Tillingham, a small village situated within the vicinity of Maldon. Despite its limited local amenities, it boasts a picturesque location with a few local pubs, a mobile library, a mobile fish and chip van, and a local post office/grocery shop. The preferred mode of transport is driving, as there is very limited public transport available.

INTRODUCTION

Swan Care Home is set in the tranquil Village of Tillingham within the county of Essex. We have 18 single rooms and 1 double room for Elderly people and people with Dementia aged 65 years and over.

Welcome to Swan Care Home, Tillingham

We have a GP complete regular rounds, District Nurse teams and a visiting Chiropodist. We also have other visiting professionals for example Dieticians, Physiotherapists, if needed to ensure the best quality of life for our service users.

We have activities schedule that takes into account all of our service users' individual requirements. Activities are planned individually and in groups. Some service users enjoy a chat, cup of tea and activities with people that live in the Village. We also arrange outings throughout the year and the Christmas Pantomime is always a favourite. We arrange fundraising events to raise funds for service user trips but also other charities such as McMillan and the Alzheimers Society.

ABOUT OUR STAFF

All of our staff at Swan Care Home are trained in Dementia Care to enable the specialist care needed and staff provide compassionate care that is focused around the individual needs of each service users to ensure they are treated with dignity and respect and remain as independent as possible.
